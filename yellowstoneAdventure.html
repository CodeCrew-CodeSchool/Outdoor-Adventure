Hiking in Yellowstone

"The Uncle Tom's Trail is a short .6 mile trail that descends quickly down 328 stairs to get an amazing view of Lower Yellowstone Falls. While this trail is short, the 8,000+ elevation makes this trail a challenge as you climb back up all the stairs. This trail is located in the Northern section of Yellowstone National Park within the "Grand Canyon of Yellowstone." This hike is a must if you visit Yellowstone!" -Youtube video description
